# PLANOVA DEVELOPMENT PRINCIPLES

## Principle 1

Architecture First

Never sacrifice architecture for speed.

---

## Principle 2

Specification Before Code

Every feature starts with a specification.

---

## Principle 3

Build Small

Implement incrementally.

Avoid big bang development.

---

## Principle 4

Module Isolation

Keep modules loosely coupled.

---

## Principle 5

Localization First

All UI text must support localization.

---

## Principle 6

AI Provider Independence

Never lock into a single AI provider.

---

## Principle 7

Automation Independence

Never lock into a single automation platform.

---

## Principle 8

Database First

Database is the source of truth.

---

## Principle 9

Testability

Business logic must be testable.

---

## Principle 10

Observability

Every major operation must be logged.

---

## Principle 11

Performance

Async by default.

Avoid blocking operations.

---

## Principle 12

Enterprise Ready

Design every feature with future enterprise deployment in mind.

---

## Principle 13

Documentation

Document architecture decisions.

Document public interfaces.

Document module responsibilities.

---

## Principle 14

Build vs Buy

Prefer proven solutions for commodity functionality.

Focus engineering effort on proprietary value.

---

## Principle 15

Long-Term Vision

Every decision should support the future evolution of Planova into a full Project Intelligence Platform.
